The criminalization of the substance was for invalid reasons


TheWeedProject is a non-profit organization dedicated to advocating for the legalization of marijuana in countries around the globe.

We firmly believe that the criminalization of the substance was for invalid reasons such as political gain and racism rather than for valid reasons such as mental health, physical health, or general safety.

It is our goal to raise awareness for this issue via promotional events, campaigns, and starting conversations. We believe we can encourage citizens and governments alike to embrace the legalization of marijuana, in turn benefiting their societies.
